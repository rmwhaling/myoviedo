    • World Market at the Park - NEW EVENT!
      Center Lake Park
    Saturday
    Nov
    23
    8:00am - 1:00pm

    Discover a world of unique crafts, accessories, and vibrant flavors at the "World Market at the Park" on November 23, 2024. Held at the picturesque Center Lake Park in Oviedo, FL, this exciting event runs from 8 AM to 1 PM and features an array of vendors offering goods from around the globe. Stroll through the market, explore handmade crafts, find one-of-a-kind accessories, and sample fresh fruits and spices. Whether you're shopping for the holidays or just looking for a fun family outing, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Don’t miss this opportunity to experience the diversity of cultures while enjoying a beautiful day at the lake. Bring the whole family and immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of Oviedo on the Park!

    • Food Truck Thursday
      Center Lake Park
    Thursday
    Nov
    21
    5:00pm - 9:00pm

    The City of Oviedo presents Food Truck Thursday at Center Lake Park 5pm-9pm. Bring your own chairs and blankets and enjoy a wide variety of food truck options. This is a Free event, all ages are welcome. Food Truck Thursday is made possible by A Budget Tree Service, Waste Pro, T-Mobile, ReMax, Pat's Liquor Leaf & Wine and The Strand Apartments. Feel free to enjoy the rest of beautiful Center Lake Park during the event. There are three large open lawns, a dog park, a Veterans Tribute, splash pad, playground, and Cantina (concession stand). There is a little something for everyone at Center Lake Park! And also FREE parking!

    • Oviedo Farmers Market
      Lawton House
    Saturday
    Nov
    2
    8:00am - 1:00pm

    The market is held the 1st and 3rd Saturday of each month from 8 am to 1 pm (except July) on the grounds of the Historic Lawton House (200 W. Broadway, Oviedo) and is a “traditional style” market with produce, food, baked goods, honey, dairy, plants & orchids and related products. Over the years, we have also expanded to include various artists and artisans to bring additional products such as clothing, jewelry, wood works, and more.
