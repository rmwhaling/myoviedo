    2024 Holiday Parade
    Oviedo Blvd to Center Lake Park
    Saturday December 7, 2024

    Come join us for an unforgettable Holiday Parade with the City of Oviedo! Attendees line up along Oviedo Boulevard AND Center Lake Lane. Parade route starts at the Gym & Aquatic Center at approximately 7pm and flows through Oviedo Boulevard ending at the corner of Center Lake Lane and City Plaza! Come early and get your food from one of the many restaurants in Oviedo on the Park, then find your spot along the parade route. This is a FREE event to attend, and registration is not required.
